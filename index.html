Rich Dad Poor Dad

Robert T. Kiyosaki

"Rich Dad Poor Dad," authored by Robert T. Kiyosaki123 in 1997, alongside co-author Sharon Lechter1, is a renowned financial education book. It contrasts the financial philosophies of two father figures, imparting invaluable lessons on wealth accumulation, asset management, and financial independence. Kiyosaki's narrative challenges conventional beliefs, offering insights into building enduring financial success.
